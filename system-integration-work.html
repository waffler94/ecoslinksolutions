System Integration Work

Our custom-designed systems ensure online, continuous measurement and comply with international standards like ASME, ASTM, API, and IEC. They incorporate gas analyzers, sampling systems, probes, transport lines, and calibration gas setups.

Available in single or multi-point configurations, they support both automatic and manual calibration. Mounting options include panel, rack, cabin, shelter, or analyzer house, with technologies such as Gas Chromatograph, FT-IR, IR/NDIR, UV/NDUV, and Flame Ionization Detector (FID).

  • Custom design to customer's specifications
  • Online and continuous measurement
  • Available in compliance with international codes & standards (such as ASME, ASTM, API, IEC, etc)
  • Systems include: Gas Analyzer, Sampling Handling System, Sampling Probe, Sample Transport Line and Calibration Gas Cylinders & Rack
  • Available in single point or multi-point sampling design
  • Auto and/or Manual calibration
  • Available mounting design in Panel, Rack, Cabin, Shelter, Analyzer House
  • Analysis Technologies for Gas Chromatograph, FT-IR, IR/NDIR, UV/NDUV, Flame Ionization Detector (FID) and others
