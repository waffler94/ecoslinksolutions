Dust Analyzer

SB100

DUSTHUNTER SB100 - Dust meter based on the scattered light measurement principle
  • Description


    The DUSTHUNTER SB100 is an EN 15267 certified dust analyzer for measuring low to high concentrations, in difficult applications such as hot corrosive gases. The measurement is based on the scattered light measurement principle. The measurement is mounted on one side of the flue without requiring a so-called light trap on the other side, through automatic compensation of background light. Standard equipped with automatic checking of zero and reference points, as well as monitoring of possible contamination.

SP100

DUSTHUNTER SP100 - Approved dust gauge with measuring probe
  • Description


    The DUSTHUNTER SP100 is a suitability tested measuring instrument with measuring probe for detecting very small to medium dust concentrations. The device is also ideal for thick and double-walled pipes, because the assembly takes place on one side of the channel. The measuring method is based on the principle of forward light scattering. The device is equipped with an integrated automatic control of the zero and reference point and also registers if necessary. pollution.

  • Characteristics
    • Mounting on one side
    • For very small to medium large dust concentrations
    • Automatic check zero and reference point
    • Pollution check
    • Available with Hastelloy lance for corrosive gases
    • For small to medium size diameters
  • Benefits
    • Type approval according to: EN15267-3 <> 2001/80 / EC and 200/76 / EC (read BVA / QAL applicable)
    • Ideal for thick and double-walled pipes
    • Low maintenance due to self-monitoring and pollution control
    • Fast installation, because adjustment is not necessary
  • Applications

    • Measurements in drainage channels

Dust and Opacity Analyzer

T100

DUSTHUNTER T100 - The modular transmission dust meters
  • Description


    The modular transmission dust gauges DUSTHUNTER T with the configurations T100 and T200 are dust analyzers for medium to high concentrations of dust in gases, above the dew point. The basis for the measured value is the transmission. From the transmission value the opacity and the extinction can then be calculated, as well as the dust concentration after a gravimetric calibration. The DUSTHUNTER T100 uncludes an automatic contamination measurement and correction function. It provides users with an early warning should the contamination level on the measurement optics exceed the permitted limit.

GM32 - Measuring aggressive gases quickly and directly, even in potentially explosive atmospheres
  • Description


    The GM32 in-situ gas analyzer measures gases such as: SO2, NO, NO2 and NH3, but also the pressure and temperature in the gas pipeline - directly, quickly and without gas samples and transport. With this analyzer your process systems have measured values in real time. Thanks to the self-monitoring you get reliable measurement results at any time. And in the event of malfunctions, a warning will appear in good time.

    For an emission measurement of SO2 and NO, the GM32 has been tested for suitability in accordance with the new European standard EN 15267-3.
